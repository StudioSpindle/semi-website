Sales Play: Product Search for E-commerce

other sales plays

Challenge: One of the most important features on any e-commerce website is the search bar. Every time a potential customer can't find what they are looking for, it leads to a potentially lost sale. 

Solution: Weaviate allows searching for the context of a product rather than the keywords of a product, for example, the relation of cheddar cheese and a hamburger Weaviate can be used to improve search, automatic classification of categories, and even make relations between recipes and a customer's buying history.

Integrates with: all e-commerce solutions via the API.
