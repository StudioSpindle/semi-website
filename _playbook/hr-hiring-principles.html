Hiring principles at SeMI

At SeMI, we believe that a diverse and motivated team leads to a broad range of skills, experience, creativity and innovation. We believe in equity and inclusion. We set up our recruitment process according to the following principles:

  1. In the job advert, we put emphasis on our values and vision: we focus on what we are doing and what we want to achieve. We seek for matching values and way of working.
  2. We use gender-neutral and non-discriminatory language to avoid (unconscious) attraction- and selection bias. "Communication is not what you say, but how it's heard". In addition, there is no need for buzzwords. We focus on defining our company personality by sharing our vision, mission and values.
  3. Contact details of at least two team members are shared in the job post, so that anyone can choose who to contact.
  4. We are fully transparent in who is currently working at SeMI.
