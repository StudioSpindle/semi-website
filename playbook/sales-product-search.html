Sales Play: Solution Search for E-commerce

Challenge: When selling solutions online, search is the goto functionality customers use to find the solutions they are looking for but in 83% of the cases, customers can't find what they are looking for which might result in customers going to competitors to find the solution they are looking for. Search engines are often lacking the capabilities to tackle the last 17% of searches because people are looking for conceptual keywords (e.g., vegetables for soup) that only can be found if data is available to make these links.

Solution: SeMI's Weaviate is a semantic search engine. Besides having almost all the features traditional search engines have, it has the capabilities to understand relations thanks to its build-in machine learning model "the Contextionary" (learn more about it in the in-depth sales play about semantic search). Weaviate can automatically make relations between categories (e.g., "fruits") and solutions (e.g., "strawberries") or context (e.g., "a former politician") and solutions (e.g., "Terminator 2").

Integrates with: Any well-known e-commerce platform (e.g., Magento, Big Commerce, Prestashop, and many others) or a custom build e-commerce solution.
