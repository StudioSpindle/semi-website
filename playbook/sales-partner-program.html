Sales Partner Program

The SeMI Sales Partnership program has three tiers of which a partner can choose one;

  1. Warm referral partnership.
  2. Direct sales partnership.
  3. Recurring sales partnership.

All partners have access to the Refer a Referral program. For all three tiers the following rules are applied;

  1. The referred potential customer should be a contact that is unknown to SeMI.
  2. A referral is always an individual inside a company, not a company itself.
  3. An individual within a prospect company is always owned by one sales partner.
  4. Multiple sales partners can work with the same prospect companies.
  5. After each introduction meeting, a confirmation email will be sent to determine the type of referral.
  6. Access to Slack and WhatsApp groups.
  7. A partner’s name and contact details to be added to our website and communication channels.
  8. Partnership contracts are reviewed by two SeMI directors and confirmed via DocuSign.
  9. A partnership starts when the partnership contract is signed.
  10. SeMI is responsible for providing information to construct a use case, this information will always be delivered through the website.
  11. A sales partner is responsible for defining the case.
  12. SeMI branded logos, slide decks, etcetera will be made available to the sales partner.
  13. All deals are per use case.
  14. Partnerships will be reviewed every 6 months.
  15. A sales partner introduction that leads to a SeMI partnership is not part of the agreement.

Direct Sales Partnership

A direct sales partner (DSP) is similar to the warm referral partnership (WRP) but with the distinction that a DSP stays part of the sales process until a signed contract.

Recurring Sales Partnership

A recurring sales partner (RSP) is similar to the direct sales partnership, with the difference that the RSP’s commission is based on recurring revenue.

Refer a Referral

The refer a referral (RaR) program is available to every partner who introduces SeMI to a new sales partner, regardless of the tier, will receive a bonus percentage when this new partner conducts a sale.
