Solution Logos and Brands

While rooted in the overall SeMI brand, each SeMI solution has its own brand identity. Their main expression are solution icons. These are constructed using planes and lines on the base grid, generating three-dimensional looking items that reference the solution.

Weaviate

An abstracted version of Weaviate’s ‘W’ is constructed using planes and lines that simulate a volumetric figure.

Weaviate uses the Indigo and Lime colors of SeMI’s color palette. Depending on the intended use, there are different versions of the logo available.

SeMI uses also distinct logos for the Open Source and Enterprise versions.
