Partner Program

SeMI works with three types of partnership;

SeMI Reseller Partnership (SRP)
A reseller partnership is a partnership with a company implementing Weaviate with end-customers.

SeMI Sales Partnership (SSP) A sales partnership is with individuals providing warm-referral use cases.

SeMI Strategy Partnership (STrP) A strategy partnership is with a consultancy firm using Weaviate technology to execute on business consultancy.
