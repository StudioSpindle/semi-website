What is SeMI?

SeMI is a Knowledge Network of companies, who want enrich their market and customer insights by collaborating with each other.

Members make valuable, but privacy insensitive, metadata available to the network. At the same time they develop their custom use cases, which leverage insights generated by the whole network.

Knowledge is shared and made available to all members.

Members spend SeMI Coins to ask questions and earn SeMI Coins when their knowledge contributes to the creation of insights.

SeMI Score

SeMI adds value to the network by cross-referencing different knowledge sources and expanding the amount of linked data. We do this by applying the SeMI Score.

Control & Security

SeMI is a decentralised network. This means each member keeps full control over their data.

The decision of what to expose to the network and what the security measures are is entirely left to each member.

Speed & High Value Insights

SeMI enable complex queries in real-time by utilising the semantic web technology. From a technical perspective SeMI is a giant network of knowledge graphs.

Who is SeMI?

SeMI is a startup funded by the ING Accelerator in Amsterdam.
