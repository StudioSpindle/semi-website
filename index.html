Get the insights you need and explore what’s possible

Can you make sense of your data?

Let’s admit it – If your company is like most, you are having difficulty gaining information and insights from your data. At SeMI, we believe natural language processing can give meaning and context to your data.

SeMI is a decentralized network, where members can exchange knowledge based on the insights hidden in their metadata. SeMI is a unique network, where you as a member can receive customized and real time insights, which are unavailable elsewhere. For additional insights, you can even build your own application on top of SeMI.

Enrich your knowledge and insights

SeMI uses natural language processing to help you make sense of your data in a way that makes sense to you. Natural language processing focuses on the interactions between human language and computers.

SeMI is a decentralized semantic knowledge network, where members exchange knowledge based on metadata. This enables you to exponentially enrich your insights about your customers and market. SeMI focuses on the things that the raw data portrays and links that information together to create a knowledge graph.

The power of SeMI is its ability to connect multiple systems and sources of information without a common information structure. Different systems and sources do not need to know about each other’s dataset and the way that the information is stored. Everyone’s data is secured.

SeMI empowers you to make better decisions

SeMI offers two products: SeMI Local and SeMI World. To generate meaningful insights from your own data, start with SeMI Local. To dramatically enhance the quality of your knowledge and insights, go straight to SeMI World.
