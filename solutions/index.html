Goal of this section: Answers the question what point solutions are available, and what point solutions are.

 

In Forbes 500, 95% of CTO's mention that with the data they have they can't solve a business problem. Put the data in Weaviate and build on top of that. (restructure data)

 

New way of looking at data: Capturing all the data from the research, that works. Next step: "We have all these fields which is organised in tables. This is where Weaviate comes in. When you want to organize the data differently, and make other connections, this enables Weaviate.

 

Point solutions are created to solve one specific problem. Point solutions are focused and therefore are best in class examples in their field. You can make use of pre-existing point solutions which have proven value.

 

"This is done at the API level. Not in the UI. The UI level, is client specific."
