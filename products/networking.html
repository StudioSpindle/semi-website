SeMI Network Nodes

Besides Weaviate allowing you to customly build knowledge networks, you can also tap into exsisting knowledge network with nodes containing data that is commonly used. The networking product Keyper allows you to manage your networks.

Currently available knowledge networks include;

Common Knowledge Network-node

Fed by the largest encyclopedia in the world, WikiPedia, the Common Knowledge Network gives insights about everything ranging from cities to businesses.

Small and medium-sized Enterprises Network-node

The SME network contains an evergrowing knowledge graph of public SME data. Ideal for enhancing your own data sets with .

Weaviate Keyper

Govern your custom networks with Keyper

Keyper is a simple tool that helps you to create and manage networks. You can create keys for your peers and with Weaviate Whitelabel Platform you can monitize the network queries if desired.
