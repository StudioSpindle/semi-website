{% include molecule-section-heading.html title='Core Features' %}

Semantic Search

Weaviate's Contextionary (the core machine learning-powered engine) is not only able to find the data your looking for fast and reliable but also to index it based on its meaning.

Automatic Classification

Weaviate helps to automatically classify data together based on the meaning of the data, even if your data contains complex branch-specific language! Weaviate can organize it according to your wishes, 100% automated.

Knowledge Representation

Weaviate allows you to represent your data in a large graph format based on the GraphQL query language. Regardless, if you need straight ahead search engine capabilities or want a full-fledged knowledge graph, Weaviate is here to help.
