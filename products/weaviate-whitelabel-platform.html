Weaviate White-Label Platform

Build and monetize your own Weaviate Network.

Weaviate meshes all your data and makes it available as one seamless source of knowledge for contextualized research, reporting, and re-use

The White Label Platform version is based on the Weaviate software and we will configure it however you want. We can create custom Contextionaries for you, and we will work with your own team to add in whatever customisations you need. We also provide you with monetization tools to generate reveniew with your Weaviate. With that, we provide an SLA, large scale deployment in your own cloud or on premise and support.

An enterprise ready solution

Support for different clouds

Weaviate Enterprise supports multiple cloud vendors including; Google, AWS, Azure and Oracle Cloud.

Pricing per function

With Weaviate Enterprise you are charged per function via the Weaviate API. This way you only pay for what you need.

Playground

Weaviate's Playground is a graphical user interface that enables you to gather insights from the knowledge graph directly. Users of Weaviate Open Source can use the Playground on this websites, and Weaviate Enterprise users receive a custom version they can run wherever suited.

Predefined datasets

You will be able to plug in a selection of premium, predefined, knowledge graphs which can enrich your results and enhance your outcomes. These are accessed through the Weaviate Networks toolkit.
