Make better decisions with SeMI

SeMI helps you make sense of your raw data. When you combine several sources of data, you dramatically enhance the quality of your knowledge and insights. You can choose SeMI Local or SeMI World.

  1. SeMI Local

    Generate meaningful insights from your data

    SeMI Local allows you to uncover the hidden connections between different sources of information within your company. We will show you how to organize and structure your data into a knowledge graph. And how to query your knowledge graph in real time.

  2. SeMI World

    Enrich your insights exponentially with meta data from others

    In addition to generating insights from your own data, SeMI World enables you to enrich your insights by connecting to a VIP network of corporates. Use third party metadata to enhance the value of your insights. Since members only share metadata, you are always in control of your datasets and privacy.
