Background patterns

Background patterns can be used for decoration.

Using a pre-defined class for a section with a predefined use-case or when you use the SEMI styleguide SCSS you can use a background pattern simply by importing the predefined mixins.

Hexagon background pattern

The hexagon background pattern is used decoratively.
